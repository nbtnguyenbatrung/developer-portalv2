# Digital Payment Platform

# Overview

NAPAS DPP is a technical infrastructure for connecting, transmitting and processing electronic data of NAPAS's online payment transactions. NAPAS DPP connects customers and payment acceptance units with banks, foreign bank branches, financial companies licensed to issue credit cards,
and payment intermediary service providers to support customers in making payments in e-commerce transactions, 
electronic bill payments and other electronic payment services.

## Features

- Pay by card/account
- Pay/top up by card token/account token
- Pay by VietQRPay code
- Pay by Bank/Payment Intermediary application
- Pay by ApplePay
- 3DS authentication solution
